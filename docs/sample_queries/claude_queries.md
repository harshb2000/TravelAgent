# Travel Agent Query Test Set

## Experience-led

- Where can I learn to surf?
- Where can I enjoy authentic Italian lifestyle and cuisine?
- I want to see the northern lights
- I want a trip that feels like a Studio Ghibli movie
- Where can I go stargazing away from city lights?
- I want to eat at a Michelin-starred restaurant but not in Paris

## Budget-constrained

- What are underrated hidden gems I can travel to within $1000?
- I have 3 days and ₹15,000 — where can I go from Bengaluru?
- Plan a honeymoon in Europe for under $3000 for two people
- What is the cheapest time of year to fly to Japan?
- Travel Southeast Asia for a month, absolute minimum spend

## Constraint-heavy

- I'm vegan, travelling with a 5-year-old, and need wheelchair access. Plan 4 days in Rome.
- I have a severe nut allergy. Where in Asia is safest to travel?
- I can only travel on long weekends this year — suggest 12 trips from Delhi
- My partner hates beaches but I love them. Plan a compromise trip.
- Visa-free destinations from Indian passport under $800
- No flights — only trains and ferries — London to Istanbul

## Itinerary requests

- Plan 7 days in Japan for first-time visitors
- Road trip from San Francisco to Portland — what should I see?
- I have a 9-hour layover in Dubai — what can I do?
- Visit a different country every day for 5 days starting in Amsterdam
- Redo my itinerary but cut Monday — I need to fly back early

## Timing & seasonality

- When is the best time to visit Rajasthan?
- I want to see cherry blossoms — where and when?
- I want to attend the Rio Carnival — plan the full trip
- Which of my saved destinations is best to visit this December?

## Ambiguous / conversational

- I want to travel somewhere nice
- Book me a trip to Bali next month
- Something similar to what I did last year
- I just need a break
- Is this a good time to visit Morocco?

## Safety & logistics

- Is it safe to travel to Pakistan right now?
- Do I need any vaccinations for Tanzania?
- Entry requirements for Japan on an Indian passport
- My flight got cancelled mid-trip — what are my options?
- What currency should I carry in Vietnam and how much?
